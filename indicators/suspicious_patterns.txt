# Suspicious Social Media Behavioral Patterns
# Used for OSINT profiling and account threat analysis

## High-Risk Account Signals
- Account created less than 30 days ago posting urgent content
- No profile picture or generic/stock photo avatar
- Username with random number suffix (user_48271983)
- Follower count near zero but high posting frequency
- Bio contains vague phrases: "making money online", "DM for opportunities"
- Only shares/reposts — no original content
- Posting identical content across multiple groups

## High-Risk Post Signals
- ALL CAPS with excessive exclamation marks
- Shortened URLs with zero context
- "DM for details" combined with vague financial offer
- Requesting personal information in public replies
- Claims of official affiliation without platform verification badge
- Emotional manipulation: fear ("your account will be deleted"), greed ("free money")

## Bot Behavior Patterns
- Multiple identical posts within minutes
- Replies to trending hashtags with unrelated phishing links
- Post/delete cycles after recording link clicks
- Coordinated posting with other suspicious accounts

## Infrastructure Red Flags
- Domain registered within 7 days of campaign start
- SSL certificate from free provider (Let's Encrypt on suspicious domain)
- Hosting on bulletproof providers
- Multiple similar domains registered simultaneously
